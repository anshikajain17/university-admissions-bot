B.Tech Admission Requirements:

Eligibility:
Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
Minimum 60% aggregate marks required.
Admission based on JEE Main score.

Documents Required:
- 10th Marksheet
- 12th Marksheet
- JEE Scorecard
- Aadhaar Card
- Passport Size Photographs

Application Process:
1. Register online
2. Fill application form
3. Upload documents
4. Pay application fee
5. Submit form

Important Dates:
Application starts in March.
Last date to apply is June 30.
Counselling begins in July.
